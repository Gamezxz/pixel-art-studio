# Study: chibi JRPG cast (Genshin-style fan sheet)

- source: pinterest a5dcf79dfcd92622950e41d704057ae7.jpg (1080px, ~6x upscale of true pixels)
- true size per character: ~26x36 px | colors: ~25-30 per character
- ramps: hair carries the identity — 3-4 step ramp, strong hue-shift (gold hair shades
  toward orange/brown, not gray); skin 2 steps + 1 blush; clothes 3 steps
- outline: selective (selout) — dark warm purple/brown per material, NOT uniform black;
  outline dissolves inside the sprite, hard only on the silhouette
- dithering: none — flat clusters with hard steps
- shading: light top-front; face almost flat (1 shadow row under fringe),
  volume lives in the hair blobs and hat

## Rules to apply
- head = ~45-50% of total height; eyes 2-3 px tall, placed low on the face
- silhouette first: hat/hair shapes read at 1x before any interior detail
- max ~28 colors; every material gets exactly 3 values (base / shade / rim)
- selout: silhouette outline = darkest value of the material it borders
- feet tiny (2px), grounded with a 1px darker contact row — no drop shadow blob

## Do NOT copy
- flat #78af75 background (present only for the sheet)
- some cells have orphan AA pixels around hair spikes from the upscale — despeckle
